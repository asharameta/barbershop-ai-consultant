GENTLEMEN BARBER SHOP — BRAND OVERVIEW

ABOUT THE BRAND
Gentlemen Barber Shop is the largest and most recognizable barber shop chain in Poland. The brand was founded in 2020 from a single small salon. Following the success of the first location, the idea of building a nationwide franchise network was born. Today the network spans over 50 salons across the country and continues to grow, with over 300 certified barbers operating under the Gentlemen name.

The brand has accumulated over 30,000 positive reviews and is widely considered unrivaled in the Polish barber market in terms of scale, consistency, and quality of service.

MISSION & VALUES
Gentlemen Barber Shop was built on the belief that every man deserves a place where the cut is excellent, the atmosphere is welcoming, and the experience leaves him feeling his best. The brand aims to be synonymous with high-quality barber services and a unique atmosphere where every man feels special.

Barbering at Gentlemen is treated as both a craft and an art — barbers don't just cut hair, they advise clients on how to care for their hair and beard at home, help them choose the right products, teach styling techniques, and propose the best solutions for each client's individual needs.

WHAT SETS GENTLEMEN APART
- Largest barber network in Poland (50+ salons, 300+ barbers)
- Consistent quality standards enforced across all franchise locations
- Gentlemen Barber Academy: in-house training school for new barbers
- Full-service approach: haircuts, beard work, straight razor shaving, wax, Face SPA
- Online booking available at all locations
- Friendly, professional atmosphere designed to make every client feel at home
