GENTLEMEN BARBER SHOP — FRANCHISE INFORMATION
Source: gentlemenbarber.pl/franczyza, franchising.pl, wiadomoscihandlowe.pl, search data

OVERVIEW
Gentlemen Barber Shop offers a franchise model that allows entrepreneurs to open their own salon under the Gentlemen brand. No prior experience in the barber or beauty industry is required — the brand provides full training, support, and know-how from day one.

The brand is actively expanding its network of salons across Poland, prioritizing quality and consistency in every new location.

WHO CAN OPEN A GENTLEMEN FRANCHISE?
No barber experience necessary. The brand trains barbers from scratch through the Gentlemen Barber Academy. What matters most is engagement, willingness to learn, and a passion for working with people. Franchisees who prefer not to cut hair themselves can take on a pure management/owner role.

LOCATION REQUIREMENTS
- Minimum salon size: 35–40 m²
- Minimum 4 barber stations required
- Available in cities with populations of 20,000 and above
- The brand helps franchisees find and evaluate suitable premises

INVESTMENT COSTS
Total estimated investment: approximately 90,000–120,000 PLN
- One-time license fee: 25,000 PLN net
- Salon fit-out and equipment: remainder of total budget (~65,000–95,000 PLN)
Own capital required: approximately 90,000–100,000 PLN
Monthly franchise royalty fee: 8% of gross revenue

RETURN ON INVESTMENT
Fastest recorded ROI: 6 months
Longest recorded ROI: 18 months
Typical range: several months to approximately 1.5 years

WHAT FRANCHISEES RECEIVE
- Gentlemen Barber Shop brand name, identity, and reputation
- Full salon design and fit-out concept (based on proven implementations)
- Assistance with location selection
- Initial staff recruitment — the brand conducts the first recruitment drive
- Full barber training through Gentlemen Barber Academy (barbers are trained from zero; the brand prefers to train people without prior experience so no bad habits are carried over)
- Ongoing marketing support (digital marketing, influencer partnerships, themed events, local promotions)
- Ongoing mentoring and business advisory
- Continuous training and development opportunities
- Access to the brand's operational know-how and systems

GENTLEMEN BARBER ACADEMY (for franchise staff)
All barbers working under the Gentlemen brand are trained at the Gentlemen Barber Academy. Courses run in 5 cities: Rzeszów, Kielce, Katowice, Łódź, and Wrocław. The full course costs 12,000 PLN and includes practical and theoretical training, work with live models, professional tools during training, and instruction on client communication and working under time pressure.
