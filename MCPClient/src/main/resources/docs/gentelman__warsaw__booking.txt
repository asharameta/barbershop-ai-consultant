GENTLEMEN BARBER SHOP — BOOKING & APPOINTMENTS

HOW TO BOOK A VISIT

Option 1 — Online via Booksy
Booksy is the primary online booking platform used across most Gentlemen locations.
Available 24/7. You can see real-time availability, choose a specific barber, select your service, and book directly.
Find your location: booksy.com → search "Gentlemen Barber Shop" + your city
Or go directly to: www.gentlemenbarber.pl and follow the booking link for your nearest salon.

Option 3 — By phone
Each salon has its own phone number. Examples:
- Łódź Cedry: 733 515 715
- Łódź Wspólna: 730 515 315
- Łódź Składowa: 886 515 959
- Kraków Dietla: +48 732 126 188
For other locations, find the number on the salon's Booksy page or via gentlemenbarber.pl.

Option 4 — Walk-in
Walk-ins are welcome at all locations. Waiting times vary — weekdays are generally shorter, weekends (especially Saturday mornings) can be busier. Booking in advance is recommended to guarantee your preferred time slot and barber.

CHOOSING A BARBER
When booking online, you can typically browse available barbers at a location and select a specific one. If you have a preferred barber, it's recommended to book their specific slot rather than arriving as a walk-in.

CANCELLATIONS & CHANGES
Cancellation policies vary by location. In general, if you need to cancel or reschedule, do so as early as possible via the same platform (Booksy) you used to book, or by calling the salon directly.

WHAT TO EXPECT AT YOUR FIRST VISIT
When you arrive, the barber will consult with you about your preferred style, face shape, and how you normally maintain your hair or beard. First-time clients are encouraged to bring reference photos of their desired style. The barber will also advise on home care products and maintenance between visits.
