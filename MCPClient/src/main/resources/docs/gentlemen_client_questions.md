# Gentlemen Barber Shop — Client Questions for AI Consultant
*Sample questions a client might ask, grouped by expected answer type*

---

## ✅ CLEAR YES — Questions where the answer is definitively YES

**Q1.** "Can I book a specific barber I like, or is it random?"
> **Answer: YES** — You can choose your preferred barber when booking online via Booksy or Fresha.

**Q2.** "Do you offer a package for a dad and his son?"
> **Answer: YES** — The Father & Son package starts from 150 PLN. There's also a Bearded Father & Son version from 190 PLN.

**Q3.** "After the service, can the barber recommend products for home care?"
> **Answer: YES** — Advising on home grooming routines and products is a core part of every Gentlemen barber's role.

**Q4.** "Is it possible to cover gray hair at your salon?"
> **Answer: YES** — Gray coverage is available as a standalone colour service and as part of several combo packages.

**Q5.** "Can I walk in without an appointment?"
> **Answer: YES (at most locations)** — Walk-ins are welcome at most Gentlemen salons, though booking ahead is recommended on weekends and evenings.

---

## ❌ CLEAR NO — Questions where the answer is definitively NO

**Q6.** "Is beard trimming included in the price of a haircut?"
> **Answer: NO** — Beard trimming is a separate service starting from 60–70 PLN. Combo packages are available if you want both.

**Q7.** "Do you do haircuts for toddlers and young children under 7?"
> **Answer: NO** — Kids' haircuts are available for children aged 7–12 years only.

**Q8.** "Are all your salons open on Sundays?"
> **Answer: NO** — Most locations are closed on Sundays. A small number open from 10:00–15:00. Always check your specific salon on Booksy.

**Q9.** "Can I pay the same price at every Gentlemen location?"
> **Answer: NO** — Prices are listed as starting prices. The final cost may vary by location, city, and service complexity.

**Q10.** "Do I need to bring my own grooming products to the appointment?"
> **Answer: NO** — All tools and professional products are provided by the salon. Just show up — optionally with a reference photo if you have a specific style in mind.

---

## 🤔 IT DEPENDS — Questions where the answer is nuanced or varies

**Q11.** "How much will my haircut cost?"
> **It depends** on your location and what service you choose. A standard haircut starts from 80–90 PLN, but prices vary by city and salon. Check your local Booksy listing for exact pricing.

**Q12.** "How long will my appointment take?"
> **It depends** on the service. A haircut alone is shorter; a full combo (haircut + beard + straight razor shave) takes significantly longer. Ask when booking or check the service listing on Booksy/Fresha.

**Q13.** "Can I get a haircut on a Sunday?"
> **It depends** on the location. Most salons are closed Sundays, but some open 10:00–15:00. Check your nearest salon via Booksy or gentlemenbarber.pl.

**Q14.** "Is the Face SPA service available at your salon?"
> **It depends** on the specific location. Face SPA is available at select locations, not all. Check your local salon's service menu on Booksy or Fresha.

**Q15.** "How soon can I get an appointment?"
> **It depends** on the day, time, and which barber you want. Popular slots (evenings and weekends) fill up fast. Booking in advance is recommended, especially for a specific barber.
