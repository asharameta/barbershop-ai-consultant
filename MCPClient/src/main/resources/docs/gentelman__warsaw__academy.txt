ABOUT THE ACADEMY
Gentlemen Barber Academy is the official training school of Gentlemen Barber Shop — the in-house barber education program created to supply the growing franchise network with skilled, brand-trained barbers. The academy was built on the philosophy that anyone can become a great barber with the right training, regardless of prior experience.

The academy is also open to individuals who want to pursue a career in barbering independent of the franchise.

COURSE LOCATIONS
Training is conducted in 5 cities across Poland:
- Rzeszów
- Kielce
- Katowice
- Łódź
- Wrocław

COURSE DETAILS
Course type: Intensive barber course (from zero / beginner to professional)
Full course price: 12,000 PLN
Format: Combination of practical and theoretical training

What's included:
- Access to professional tools and equipment during the entire course
- Hands-on work with live models (not just mannequins)
- Instruction in cutting techniques, fade techniques, beard sculpting, shaving, and styling
- Training in proper client approach, communication, and consultation
- Practice working under real time pressure (as in a live salon environment)
- Support from experienced instructors

WHO IS IT FOR?
- Aspiring barbers with zero prior experience (the preferred candidate profile for the Gentlemen network)
- Existing barbers wanting to upgrade their skills
- Entrepreneurs opening a Gentlemen franchise who want their staff trained to brand standards
- Anyone seeking a career change into men's grooming

BRAND PHILOSOPHY ON TRAINING
Gentlemen Barber Shop intentionally prefers to train barbers without prior experience. The reasoning: those without prior habits are easier to develop into barbers who meet the brand's specific quality standards. The academy's motto reflects this — every barber is built from the ground up to Gentlemen's standard.
