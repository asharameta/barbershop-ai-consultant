# Gentlemen Barber Shop — Quiz Questions

---

## ✅ CATEGORY 1: FULLY TRUE
*(All answers are confirmed TRUE based on brand materials)*

**Q1.** Gentlemen Barber Shop was founded in 2020 from a single small salon.
> **Answer: TRUE** — The brand was founded in 2020 from one location and grew into a nationwide franchise network.

---

**Q2.** The Gentlemen Barber Academy runs training courses in 5 cities across Poland.
> **Answer: TRUE** — Training is conducted in Rzeszów, Kielce, Katowice, Łódź, and Wrocław.

---

**Q3.** The one-time franchise license fee for opening a Gentlemen salon is 25,000 PLN.
> **Answer: TRUE** — The brand charges a one-time license fee of 25,000 PLN net, in addition to fit-out costs.

---

**Q4.** Beard trimming is a separate service and is NOT included in the standard haircut price.
> **Answer: TRUE** — Beard trimming starts from 60–70 PLN and is charged separately. Combo packages are available.

---

**Q5.** Gentlemen Barber Shop has accumulated over 30,000 positive reviews.
> **Answer: TRUE** — Stated in the brand overview as a key measure of customer satisfaction.

---

## ❌ CATEGORY 2: FULLY FALSE
*(All answers are confirmed FALSE — these are common misconceptions or deliberate errors)*

**Q6.** The Gentlemen Barber Shop brand headquarters is located in Warsaw.
> **Answer: FALSE** — The headquarters is in **Rzeszów** (Krogulskiego 37/8/2, 35-233 Rzeszów), not Warsaw.

---

**Q7.** The monthly franchise royalty fee is 15% of gross revenue.
> **Answer: FALSE** — The royalty fee is **8%** of gross revenue, not 15%.

---

**Q8.** The Gentlemen Barber Academy full course costs 8,000 PLN.
> **Answer: FALSE** — The full course costs **12,000 PLN**, not 8,000 PLN.

---

**Q9.** Franchise applicants must have prior experience in the barber or beauty industry.
> **Answer: FALSE** — No barber experience is required. The brand trains all staff from scratch and actually prefers candidates without prior experience.

---

**Q10.** Kids' haircuts at Gentlemen are available for children aged 3–6 years.
> **Answer: FALSE** — Kids' haircuts are available for children aged **7–12 years**.

---

## 🔄 CATEGORY 3: IT DEPENDS / 50–50
*(These questions have no simple yes/no answer — the truth is nuanced)*

**Q11.** Gentlemen Barber Shop salons are closed on Sundays.
> **Answer: IT DEPENDS** — Most locations are closed on Sundays, but some locations open from 10:00–15:00. Always confirm with your specific salon via Booksy or the website.

---

**Q12.** A standard haircut at Gentlemen Barber Shop costs 80 PLN.
> **Answer: IT DEPENDS** — Prices *start from* 80–90 PLN, but the final price varies by location, city, and service complexity. The same haircut could cost more at some salons.

---

**Q13.** You can walk into any Gentlemen salon without booking.
> **Answer: IT DEPENDS** — Walk-ins are welcome at *most* locations, but not necessarily all. Booking in advance is recommended, especially on weekends and evenings.

---

**Q14.** A franchise investor can recoup their investment within 6 months.
> **Answer: IT DEPENDS** — 6 months is the *fastest recorded* ROI, but the typical range is several months to 18 months. It is possible, but not guaranteed.

---

**Q15.** Opening a Gentlemen franchise requires a minimum salon size of 35 m².
> **Answer: IT DEPENDS** — The requirement is 35–40 m². A 35 m² space might be acceptable at the lower end, but a 34 m² space would not meet the minimum. The answer depends on exact interpretation.
