GENTLEMEN BARBER SHOP — FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Source: gentlemenbarber.pl, search data, Booksy listings, blog content

Q: How do I book an appointment?
A: You can book online 24/7 via Booksy — search for "Gentlemen Barber Shop" plus your city on either platform. You can also call your local salon directly (numbers are listed on each salon's Booksy page and on gentlemenbarber.pl). Walk-ins are welcome at all locations, though booking in advance is recommended for weekends and evenings.

Q: Can I choose a specific barber?
A: Yes. When booking online via Booksy or Fresha, you can view the available barbers at your chosen location and select the one you prefer. If you have a favourite barber, booking their slot directly is always the best way to guarantee availability.

Q: Do I need to prepare anything before my visit?
A: No special preparation is needed. If you have a specific style in mind — especially for a first visit — it helps to bring a reference photo. Your barber will consult with you at the start of the appointment about your preferences, face shape, hair type, and lifestyle before starting any work.

Q: How much does a haircut cost?
A: Prices start from around 80–90 PLN for a standard haircut. Combo services (haircut + beard) start from around 120 PLN. Prices may vary slightly between locations. For the exact price list at your nearest salon, check its Booksy page, or visit gentlemenbarber.pl.

Q: Is beard trimming included in a haircut?
A: No — beard trimming is a separate service starting from around 60–70 PLN. However, combo packages combining a haircut and beard service are available and offer better value. Ask your barber or check the service menu at your location.

Q: What is a hot towel shave / straight razor shave?
A: A straight razor shave uses a traditional folding blade for an extremely close shave. The hot towel preparation softens the skin and hair before the shave, making it more comfortable and leaving skin very smooth. It starts from around 80 PLN and is available at all Gentlemen locations.

Q: Do you offer services for kids?
A: Yes. Kids' haircuts are available for children aged 7–12 years, starting from around 80–90 PLN. Barbers are experienced in working patiently with younger clients.

Q: Is there a Father & Son package?
A: Yes. The Father & Son package covers a full cut for the father and a tailored cut for his son in one session, starting from around 150 PLN. A Bearded Father & Son package (which adds a full beard service for the father) starts from around 190 PLN.

Q: Can you cover gray hair?
A: Yes. Gray coverage is available as a standalone colour service (from ~80 PLN) and as part of several combo packages. Ask your barber about the best approach for your hair type and the degree of coverage you want.

Q: What is Face SPA?
A: Face SPA is a professional deep cleansing facial treatment available at Gentlemen salons, starting from around 70 PLN. It's a grooming service focused on facial skin health and care, not just hair.

Q: Do barbers give advice on home care?
A: Yes, absolutely. Advising clients on home maintenance is a core part of what Gentlemen barbers do. After your service, your barber can recommend grooming products, teach you styling techniques, and suggest a routine suited to your hair or beard type.

Q: Where are Gentlemen Barber Shop salons located?
A: The network has 50+ salons across Poland, in cities including Rzeszów, Łódź, Poznań, Wrocław, Kraków, Kielce, Lublin, Gdynia, Słupsk, Sanok, Mielec, Gliwice, Katowice, Szczecin, Dębica, and many more. Full salon list: www.gentlemenbarber.pl/nasze-salony

Q: I want to open my own Gentlemen salon. How does the franchise work?
A: The total investment is approximately 90,000–120,000 PLN. No barber experience is required — the brand trains all staff. The brand charges a one-time license fee of 25,000 PLN and a monthly royalty of 8% of revenue. ROI is typically achieved within 6–18 months. Full details: www.gentlemenbarber.pl/franczyza

Q: I want to become a barber. How do I train?
A: Gentlemen Barber Academy runs intensive barber courses in Rzeszów, Kielce, Katowice, Łódź, and Wrocław. The full course costs 12,000 PLN and includes hands-on training with live models. No prior experience needed. Website: www.gentlemenbarberacademy.pl

Q: How do I know if a barber is good?
A: According to Gentlemen Barber Shop's own content: a good barber has completed specialist training, understands current beard and hair trends, can recommend the right products for your needs, and demonstrates precision in outlining beard lines — one of the most technically demanding barber skills. You can also check reviews for your specific barber on Booksy.
